I am a Lecturer at the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Tiangong University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2024, and was a Visiting Ph.D. student at the BBCR Laboratory, University of Waterloo from 2022 to 2023.

## Research Interests

My research interests include:

- Unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) communications 
- Intelligent reflecting surfaces (AIRS) assisted communication
- Mobile edge computing (MEC) and task offloading
- Age of information (AoI) optimization
- Wireless resource allocation and network optimization
