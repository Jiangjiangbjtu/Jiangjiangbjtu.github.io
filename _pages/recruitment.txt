## Prospective Students 招生方向

I am actively looking for motivated students interested in the following directions:

- UAV-assisted wireless communications
- Aerial intelligent reflecting surfaces (AIRS)
- Mobile edge computing and task offloading
- Resource allocation and optimization

## Openings 招生名额

- **Master's students (硕士研究生)**: 2-3 positions per year
- **Undergraduate research assistants (本科科研助手)**: Open year-round

## Requirements 对学生的期望

- **Strong fundamentals** in mathematics (linear algebra, optimization, probability)
- **Programming skills**: proficiency in Python and/or MATLAB
- **Communication skills**: ability to read and write in English at an academic level
- **Self-motivation**: willing to commit to research and meet deadlines

Prior experience in wireless communications or optimization is a plus but not required for undergraduates.

## What I Offer 我能提供

- Active research funding and computing resources
- Weekly one-on-one meetings and group seminars
- Opportunities to publish in top-tier journals and conferences
- Recommendation letters for further studies (PhD applications, study abroad)
- A collaborative and supportive lab environment

## How to Apply 如何申请

Please send an email to **jww@tiangong.edu.cn** with the subject line:

> **[Research Application] Your Name - Your Affiliation**

Include the following in your email:

1. A brief introduction of yourself
2. Your CV (with GPA, ranking, and any research/project experience)
3. Why you are interested in my research
4. Any prior research outputs (papers, projects, code)

I read every email and will reply within a week.
